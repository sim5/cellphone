Return the Cellphone

  • High Concept: A dude picked up a cellphone and he tries to return the phone to the owner.
  • Player Experience Goals: Tension and carefulness to dodge the rocks and kill the attacking birds.
  • Genre: Platformer.
  • Game play: The player can only move upward using the up arrow key at the beginning. He/she can shoot by pressing the space bar. When the time is up, the player can move sideways using the left and right arrow keys to reach the owner of the phone.
  • Prototype goal: Jumping, dodging, killing, moving.
  • Features: There are two things to do at the same time. You should watch out for the approaching rocks, as well as the birds coming at you. You have to kill the birds by throwing the phone at them in order to survive.
  • Setting/Premise: It is a sunny day with clouds in the sky. There are many birds in the sky and countless rocks on the street. A poor cellphone owner has lost his cellphone but he has not realized it. A dude picked it up and chases after the owner to return it.
  • Story: One day, a dude was walking on the street and saw a stranger dropping his cellphone from his pocket. The dude picked it up and he chases after the owner to return the phone back to the owner. However, there are many rocks and birds in the way.
  • Target Audience: Any age groups who can control using keyboard can play this game.
  • Play time: Around 50 seconds.
  • Strategies: The player will have to watch for both rocks and birds at all times.
